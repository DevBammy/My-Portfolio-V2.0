About me

I am a graduate of Physics Education from Kogi State University, Anyigba, Kogi State, Nigeria. Throughout my degree up until l graduated, served and completed my NYSC, I have worked on a range of briefs including Branding, web design and many social Media Ad campaigns. During these briefs, I improved upon my team work and communication skills while developing my organization skills and self motivation by working on self innovated projects.

Working on self range of projects has helped me to gain a much deeper knowledge of each aspect that is needed for graphic/web design. This has also given me the skills needed to work in this environment including having an in-depth knowledge in areas such as branding, typography, layout adjustment and digital design.

Taking my skills further and having done a vast amount of training in software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, I have improved the quality of my work and software skills. I have also gained experience in using Visual Studio Code for web development (HTML, CSS and JavaScript).
